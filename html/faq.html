FAQ

Go to the login page, if you don't have an account, just register, then re-enter your login information, click on the "Login" button, then you will be directed to the user page where you can start working

Click on "Create New Database", a pop up menu will appear where you can enter the name of the project you are working on, then write the classes and their attributes information, and add as much as you want with their appropriate buttons, then click on the three buttons: "Generate New Database", "Create SQL File", and "Create UML"

Just click on "Delete Database" button and choose the database you want to delete from the drop down menu, and after you choose it, just click on "Delete", then your database will be successfully deleted

If you have already created a sql file, go to the "Export File" pop up menu, where you will see your created files there. There, you will have the option to export them by clicking on the button, then after that you will see them downloaded and saved onto your computer. While if you want to import user-owned files, remmember it only accepts sql and json files, so when you import the sql file, you will see it imported. Howeveer, when you import your json file, you will see it imported and also converted into sql which will be downloaded and saved onto your computer
